Passport Renewal

Complete procedure and requirements for renewing your Congolese passport.

Step-by-Step Procedure

  1. 1

    Prepare Required Documents

    Gather all necessary documents listed in the requirements section.

  2. 2

    Book an Appointment

    Schedule your visit through the online appointment booking system.

  3. 3

    Visit the Embassy

    Bring your documents and appear at the embassy on your scheduled date.

  4. 4

    Pay Fees

    Complete payment via credit card, debit card, or bank transfer.

  5. 5

    Collect Passport

    Pick up your new passport or choose the delivery option.

Required Documents

  • Current passport (original and copy of biodata page)
  • 2 recent passport photos (4x6 cm, white background)
  • Completed and signed application form
  • Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement)

Processing Information

Processing Time
4-6 Weeks
Standard service
Fees
Standard $150
Express $300
